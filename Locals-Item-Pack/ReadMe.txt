Information
-----------------------
*** Version: 1.0.0
*** FivePD Version Support: 1.0.5.1



What Is This?
-------------
A Item modification which adds more items which can be found when searching a local or their vehicle.



How To Install
--------------------------
Drag 'items.json' into your 'fivepd/config' folder. Make sure you agree to override the file!
